# Quick Start Guide ⏱️

Welcome to **Weekly Reporter**! This guide will help you get up and running in less than 5 minutes.

## 1. Prerequisites
Before you start, make sure you have these three things installed:
- [ ] **Google Chrome**: The browser used for automation.
- [ ] **Python 3.10+**: The engine that runs the app. [Download here](https://www.python.org/downloads/)
- [ ] **Ollama**: The local AI engine. [Download here](https://ollama.com/)

---

## 2. Launching the App

1. **Download the Project**: Pull the `Weekly Reporter` folder onto your computer.
2. **Double-Click to Start**:
   - **On Mac**: Double-click `Start-Mac.command`.
   - **On Windows**: Double-click `Start-Windows.bat`.
3. **Keep the Window Open**: A terminal window will open. Leave it running while you use the app!

---

## 3. Initial Setup (First Run Only)

Once the app opens in your browser:
1. **Connect to Ollama**: The app will check if Ollama is running. If not, open the Ollama app on your computer.
2. **Download a Model**: We recommend **Llama 3.2 (3B)** for most computers. Click "Download" and wait for the progress bar to finish.
3. **Save Your Profile**: Enter your **ASU ID** and name so the automation knows who you are.

---

## 4. Generating Your First Report

1. **Upload Your Tracker**: Go to the **Data Sync** tab and upload your Excel tracker (or paste your data).
2. **Generate Interactions**: Go back to the **Generator** tab, select a resident, and click **Generate**.
3. **Submit to QuestionPro**: Once you're happy with the interaction, click **Open in QuestionPro Automator**. A new Chrome window will open and fill out the form for you!

---

## ⚖️ Need Help?
If the app doesn't start, make sure you checked the box **"Add Python to PATH"** during the Windows installation.

*Happy Reporting!* 🚀
